The Importance of low-carbon Web-design in the Design Industry

Websites can generate a lot of carbon emissions. The more complicated a website, the more energy it needs, making its environmental impact worse. There are many approaches designers can take to make websites more sustainable.

    "Low carbon web design" is designing and developing websites to reduce their carbon footprint and environmental impact. It involves various strategies and considerations to minimise the energy consumption and greenhouse gas emissions used when running and accessing websites. Some of the techniques that can be used and I discuss is default text and dithering images.
    As the world's sustainability becomes increasingly important, low-carbon web design is essential, and designers need to start playing their part in saving our environment. As a designer, we can educate clients and users about the environmental impact of web design choices and raise awareness about the importance of low-carbon web design. Low-carbon web design is energy efficient and cost-efficient and is the way of the future. Designers who can incorporate sustainability principles, therefore may find themselves better positioned for the future of web design.
